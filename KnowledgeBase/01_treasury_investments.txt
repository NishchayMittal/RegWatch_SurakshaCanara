Department: Treasury & Investments
Regulator Relevance: RBI (primary), SEBI (secondary)

HANDLES:
- Capital adequacy requirements (CAR, CRAR under Basel III)
- Cash Reserve Ratio (CRR) and Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) maintenance
- Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR) and Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR) compliance
- Bond portfolio management and government securities (G-Sec) compliance
- Interest rate risk management and reporting
- Investment classification (HTM, AFS, HFT) as per RBI guidelines
- Forex treasury operations and FEMA compliance
- Derivatives policy and hedging compliance
- Reporting to RBI under XBRL/OSMOS
- ALM (Asset Liability Management) policy updates

DOES NOT HANDLE:
- KYC or customer onboarding (→ KYC/AML)
- Loan provisioning or NPA classification (→ Credit Risk)
- Equity trading compliance for customers (→ Retail Banking Compliance)
- Staff conduct or insider trading by employees (→ HR & Conduct)
- Audit of treasury operations (→ Audit & Inspection)

KEYWORDS:
CRR, SLR, LCR, NSFR, CAR, CRAR, Basel III, Tier 1, Tier 2, capital buffer,
HTM, AFS, HFT, G-Sec, government securities, bond, treasury, ALM, FEMA,
forex, derivatives, hedging, interest rate risk, XBRL, OSMOS, liquidity,
investment portfolio, repo, reverse repo, MSF, LAF, OMO

REAL REGULATORY REFERENCES:

- RBI Basel III Capital Regulations (DBR.No.BP.BC.1/21.06.201/2015-16)
